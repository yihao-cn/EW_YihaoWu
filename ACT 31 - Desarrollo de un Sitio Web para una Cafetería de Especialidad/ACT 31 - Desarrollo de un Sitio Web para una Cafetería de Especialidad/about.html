Our Story

Lit Coffee was founded by Yihao Wu in 2024 in the beautiful city of Alicante. Our goal is to create a cozy, relaxed atmosphere where you can enjoy top-quality coffee and delicious pastries. We aim for a modern yet laid-back vibe where every customer can feel at home.

Our café brings together a mix of modern style with comfort, offering an experience that appeals to both coffee aficionados and those looking for a cozy spot to relax or work.
